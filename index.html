MEET RACHEL

Rachel is a professional writer and Tibetan Buddhist scholar turned software developer.

Rachel is on an award-winning two-person team that is about to launch a SEC/FINRA compliant chat app for financial advisors. She is responsible for the fully responsive user interface, as well as some backend development. She is also excited to have recently joined Latinas in STEM, and looks forward to working with their professional development program.

You can find Rachel in Sacramento and the Bay Area teaching classes on web development and sitting on tech panels. Look for her new novel about being a developer!
